-------------------------------

Copy the URL from the address bar!

-------------------------------
Vercel might send you an email with your deployment URL.
Check your inbox for "Deployment Ready" email.

-------------------------------
1. Go to: https://vercel.com

4. Your URL is at the top of the page

-------------------------------
1. In Vercel Dashboard
2. Click "time-tracking" project
3. Click "Deployments" tab
4. Click the most recent deployment
5. Your URL is shown there

================================================================================
COMMON QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: How long should I wait?
A: 1-2 minutes after clicking "Deploy"

Q: Will I miss the URL?
A: No! It stays on the success screen. And you can always find it in your 
   Vercel Dashboard.

Q: Can I change my URL later?
A: Yes! You can add a custom domain in Vercel settings.

Q: What if deployment fails?
A: Check the build logs, fix errors, and click "Redeploy"

Q: Is the URL permanent?
A: Yes! Your URL works forever (as long as you don't delete the project)
