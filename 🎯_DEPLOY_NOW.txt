METHOD 2: Command Line (Advanced)
================================================================================

STEP 1: Login
-------------
Run this command:
vercel login

Enter your email when prompted.
Check your email and click verification link.

STEP 2: Deploy
--------------
Run this command:
vercel --prod

Follow the prompts:
- Set up and deploy? Y
- Link to existing project? N
- Project name? time-tracking
- Auto-detected settings? Y

Wait 1-2 minutes.

STEP 3: Get Your URL
--------------------
Copy the URL shown (like https://time-tracking-xyz.vercel.app)

STEP 4: Use on Phone
--------------------
On your phone, go to that URL - NO MORE 404! ‚úÖ

OR use the automated script:
